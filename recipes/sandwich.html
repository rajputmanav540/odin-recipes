Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Home


Description


Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich. Whether you're a beginner cook or an old pro, you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again.


Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps
  • Gather all ingredients.
  • Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  • Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.
  • Place the bread butter-side down in the hot skillet.
  • Add one slice of cheese on top of the bread.
  • Butter one side of a second slice of bread.
  • Place the second slice butter-side up on top of the cheese.
  • Cook until lightly browned on one side.
  • Flip the sandwich and continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
  • Repeat with the remaining slices of bread, butter, and cheese.
  • Serve and enjoy.
